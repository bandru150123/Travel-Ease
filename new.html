The Ritz-Carlton New York, Central Park boasts a prestigious address in midtown Manhattan on Central Park South between Fifth and Sixth Avenues. It’s right in the heart of an area filled with world-renowned dining and shopping opportunities, whilst Broadway’s theatres, Carnegie Hall, Lincoln Center and Rockefeller Center are just blocks away.

Formerly the landmark St. Moritz Hotel, this Ritz-Carlton has been restored and refurbished yet still captures a glimpse of history in its sophisticated decor. There are superb views over Manhattan or Central Park from many of the luxurious guest rooms and suites, all of which aim to make you feel like you're in a private townhouse. There are luxurious touches all around, including plush upholstery, brocade drapes and marble bathrooms. Suites also come with expanded living areas, perfect for relaxing and entertaining. Guests in Club Level accommodation have access to The Ritz-Carlton Club Lounge on the second floor, with complimentary food offerings including breakfast, light snacks and desserts as well as drinks and cocktails.

Just outside the hotel is the south end of Central Park, where you can easily access the park’s two skating rinks, the Central Park Zoo and the historic Belvedere Castle (a Victorian folly turned weather station). So, order a picnic from the hotel and explore. If you have the kids with you, they’ll no doubt love being in the park, and the Ritz Kids programme also provides them with colouring books and treasure hunt activities.

Visit Contour for all-day dining with American fare. Start your morning with avocado toast or a full American breakfast with two eggs and roasted potatoes, return at happy hour to learn the history of the Manhattan cocktail, and then end your evening with a slice of New York cheesecake and a glass of wine. Here in the city that never sleeps, in-room dining is, of course, available around the clock.

When you need to reset your wellbeing compass, La Prairie at The Ritz-Carlton Spa is a luxurious oasis offering a host of treatments to aid de-stressing and rejuvenation, as well as anti-ageing therapies. And there’s a fitness centre with cardiovascular and weight training equipment. Otherwise, ask for one of the city running maps from reception, and head off for a leisurely jog.

A cookie is a small text file that is stored in a dedicated location on your computer, tablet, smartphone or other devices when you use your browser to visit an online service. A cookie allows its sender to identify the device on which it is stored during the period of validity of consent.

We use cookies for purposes such as advertising, analytics and research, and to improve some of our features and services. You may accept or reject the cookies listed below. You can change your preferences and withdraw your consent at any time in your settings.
